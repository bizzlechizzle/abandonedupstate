# Al’s Toy Barn (Finds)
## Finger Lakes Region

Al's Toy Barn, located in Upstate New York's Finger Lakes region, sits near an abandoned farmhouse. Named after the scattered play toys, potential barn-find cars, and heavy equipment. Documents indicate that everything was left here in the fall of 1994.
